Bdpa is a residential area directly facing Uniben main gate, with tarred roads, a robust keke transportation system, a wide variety of stores, two shopping malls and a large number of students hostels to choose from although pricey, bdpa is a prime location for Uniben's big boys and girls.

Ebouvmore is a community just after ekosodin if one is coming from the city centre, this area also houses some number of uniben students although not as lively or busy as ekosodin.

Ekosodin is a community located just to the left of the university of benin from the city center, this area became very popular in the past partly due to it's relatively cheap accomodation when compared to other areas although prices are pretty much the same in all locations now. this area houses the largest number of uniben students and can be accessed either through the junction just after maingate or from school through faculty gate or "backgate". The huge number of student hostels and lodges, the tarred roads, keke, bus and taxi transportation, stores, restaurants and even a market this area boasts of, makes it the most convenient and most sought after residential area for students
