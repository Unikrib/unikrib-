FAQs

when uploading an apartment or product, ensure to fill in the required information like name, price, location etc. and make sure you upload atleast one photo of the product/apartment then click "save and upload" and wait for the the confirmation. if you did all these and it still didn't show please contact us via whatsapp or email and we will resolve it immediately

When trying to update your service images, make sure a preview of new photo you want to upload displays under the "upload image" button, if it doesn't that means the image hasn't been selected, if the image doesn't display after repeatedly been selected please contact us via whatsapp or email and we will resolve it immediately

Vendors are only allowed to upload one to three images of a product so vendors are unable to upload more than three images of a single product.

Agents apartment uploads are restricted to only five apartments at a time after five apartments have been uploaded, to upload a new upload the agent must first delete an existing apartment first before a new one can be uploaded

Users are only allowed to operate one account type currently, only agents can upload apartments and only vendors can upload products as well, in other to operate both account types the user must open different accounts with different emails

We tried to create broad service category for our users to choose from, but in an event where a vendor/service provider can't find his/her category he or she is advised to choose the category that closely relates with his or her category. In a case where no such category is available the user can give us feedback and we will create the category asap
