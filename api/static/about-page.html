About unikrib

Unikrib is a platform created for students in Nigerian colleges and universities. Our main goal is to make it easier for students to find vacant apartments off campus, buy products, and access domestic services. We aim to connect students to various agents, vendors, and service providers who may be students or not. Whether you're looking for a new apartment, shopping for important items, or need services like cleaning or repairs, Unikrib is here to help you do that stress free and without hassle!

Our network

Our services are currently available in the university of benin and it's environs, with plans to extend to every major university in Nigeria!

Our Mission

To improve the student experience through enhancing location based connectivity

Through our platform, we aim to provide students with a means to obtain housing, commodities, and services. That they require by connecting them to agents, vendors and service providers in their local enivironment through a medium that provides ease and eliminates difficulty.
